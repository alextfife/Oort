As you can see, you have clicked on the question mark, so you are asking yourself "What is this?" or "How does this work?" The Research tab will show all assignments that must be submitted to your History, English, and Reading Across the Genres classes, Ms. Boretsky, Mrs. Krafcik, and Ms. Sweeney (respectively).
So when you look at this tab, all the assignments in it have to be either printed or submitted (or both). This helps you find everything you need faster, and us update it more often and easily, a win-win.
